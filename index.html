Travel to Venice

Venice is a really nice city in Italy, and I really want to go there. Lets see why!

Sights and Beauty

Venice is a beautiful and romantic city, floating on the sea, with canals flowing through it.
It is gorgeous, and the old buildings give a nice, warm vibe.
You can get a good view of the city on a gondola, and the bridges also give a nice view of the canal below.
There are also the pretty streets, and large marketplaces that look good.
And at night, the city glows very beatifully.
There's not much to say about the sights of Venice, but much more to see.
So, here is a nice picture, that gives you a good view.

Delicious Food

Venice is part of Italy, and if you are familiar with Italian food, you know what this place has in store for you.
Pizza, pasta, gelato, and plenty more!
I myself am Italian, and boy, I love this food!
There are plenty of restaraunts in Venice, and maybe you can get a bite to eat at the marketplace, too.
Its hard to describe all the Italian food in venice, so before we get caught away, lets move on.
